The Weeknd

The Weeknd is a canadian singer has gained lots of popularity these past few years.
He was born February 16th 1990, and is now 33 years old. His first album came
out in 2011 named "Kiss Land" and included songs like "High For This" or "The Morning".
Some of his most popular songs are 'Blinding Lights' and 'Save Your Tears', both in the album
'After Hours' released in 2020. More popular hits are also 'Starboy' and 'Die for You' in the album
'Starboy' released in 2016.



Ariana Grande

Ariana Grande is one of the most known pop artist around the world. She is an american singer,
songwriter and actress, and has appeared in shows like 'Victorious',
'Sam and Cat' and 'The Voice'. She was born June 26th 1993 in Florida and is now 30 years old.
She also founded a popular brand called 'r.e.m. beauty', and sells makeup. Some of her
popular songs include 'Break Free' 'Problem', and '7 Rings'.



BTS

BTS is a boy band from South Korea formed in 2010, and their first song released in 2013
named 'No More Dream'. BTS consists of seven members named Jungkook, V,
Jimin, SUGA, Jin, RM and j-hope. They are very successful with some big hits such as “Butter” released
in 2021, or 'Dynamite' in 2020.
