Pop

Pop music is one of the most listened to genres of music in the world. It usually has an audience of teenager to
young adults.There have been many very popular songs over the years, such as "Call Me Maybe" by Carly Rae Jepsen,
and even "I Want it That Way" by the Backstreet Boys.



Rock

Rock music has been around for a long time. It was invented in the late 1940s and early 1950s, named Rock and Roll.
It's typically listened to by young adults but is generally listened too by everyone. Many bands and artists play
rock music, for example The Beatles, Nirvana or The Rolling Stones.



Electronic

Electronic music has become very popular nowadays. It can be created with computers, digital instuments, or electronic
musical instruments. Some popular artists are Avicii, Calvin Harris or Marshmellow. Most people that listen to this
genre of music are around 20 years old.
