ELECTRONIC MUSIC

Avicii

Avicii is a very popular Swedish DJ and has still been very popular in 2023. Tim Bergling,
known as Avicii, died April 20th 2018 to suicide. He began his careeer
at age 16, when he started posting remixes he made. Although Avicii did not sing, he
was still very popular. His first remix, named "Levels" was released in 2011
and gained instant popularity. He has had many good songs such as "Wake Me Up',
"Waiting For Love" and "Hey Brother".



David Guetta

French DJ, David Guetta was born November 7th 1967, and is now 56 years old. His
popularity and great music helped him get voted number 1 DJ in the DJ Mag Top 100
DJS poll in 2023. A very popular song released in 2023 was "I'm Good",
with Bebe Rexha. Some of his other popular songs are "Hey Mama" or "Baby Don't Hurt Me"



Tiesto

Tiesto is a Dutch Dj and record producer. He was born January 17th 1969 and is
now 55 years old. He had released many very popular songs in 2023. For example
there is "The Motto, also sung with Ava Max, "10:35" also with Tate McRae or
"The Buisness". His first album was released in 1997 named Magik One: First FLight,
and his latest album released in 2023 is called Drive.



Calvin Harris

Adam Richard Wiles, a Scottish DJ and record producer has become very popular.
Known professionally as Calvin Harris, was also born January 17th but in 1984.
Calvin Harris has MANY popular songs, some of witch are "Summer", from his
album Motion released in 2014, "One Kiss", with Dua Lipa in 2018, or "My Way",
released in 2016.
