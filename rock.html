ROCK MUSIC

Metallica

Formed in 1981, the American heavy metal rock band has 8 members. James Hetfield,
Kirk Hammett, Lars Ulrich, Cliff Burton, Robert Trujillo, Dave Mustaine,
Jason Newsted and Ron McGovney. Some of their most popular songs are
"Nothing Else Matters" released in 1991, "Master Of Puppets" released in 1986, and "Fade to Black"
released in 1984.



Foo Fighters

The Foo Fighters are a rock band formed in 1994 and have been very popular since. There
are 8 members in the band, Dave Grohl, Josh Freese, Pat Smear, Chris Shiflett, Nate Mendel,
Rami Jaffee, William Goldsmith and Franz Stahl. Their most streamed song
is "Everlong" which is part of the album The Color and the Shape, released in 1997. They
also released an album in 2023 called But Here We Are, which consists of 10 songs.



Fall Out Boy

Fall Out Boy is and American rock band formed in 2001 in Chicago. In the band there are
four members, who are Pete Wentz, Patrick Stump, Andy Hurley and Joe Trohman.
One of their most popular songs is "Centuries" released in 2014 and has gained a lot of popularity.
They also released a new album in 2023 called So Much (For) Stardust which had songs like
"We didn't start the fire" and "Love From the Other Side"



Arctic Monkeys

The popular rock band Arctic Monkeys are an English band formed in 2002. It
consists of 4 members, Alex Turner, Matt Helders, Jamie Cook and Nick O'Malley.
They have had five number one albums over the years and also gained a lot
of popularity in 2013. Some of their most famous songs are "Do I Wanna Know?",
"Why'd You Only Call Me When You're High?" and "I Wanna Be Yours"
